CASSI

Arcminute Accuracy. Real-Time Attitude. Accessible to All.

CASSI (Celestial Alignment System for Satellite Inertial-control) delivers high-precision, drift-corrected orientation data for CubeSat-class missions—at a fraction of the cost of traditional systems.

By fusing optical navigation with inertial sensing, CASSI ensures your satellite always knows where it’s pointing—whether you’re mapping Earth, aligning antennas, or navigating deep space.

Key Highlights

  • Arcminute-level accuracy with low-cost, commercial-grade components
  • Real-time quaternion output for seamless ADCS integration
  • Compact, modular design optimized for any satellite
  • Proven in simulations and real star field tests

CASSI makes high-performance attitude determination accessible to all space companies—empowering your mission with reliability, scalability, and precision.
